About Us

Established in 2019, Satay House has flourished to become a household name for good and tasty satay in Malaysia. With numerous and unbeatable support, we had succesfully operated in 3 different locations in Kedah, Selangor and Johor. Satay House is founded by two successful business entrepreneurs, Ms Lidya and Ms Shobana with first shop in Selangor. From only 3 selection of satay, customers now can enjoy 6 selection of satay to satisfy their craving.

We offer an exciting dining experience as well as lightning fast delivery to your home or office. We also cater for big and special event at your house!. What are you waiting for!
